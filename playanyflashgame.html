Find, Download, and Play Your Favorite Flash Game!

To play your favorite Flash game, follow these steps:

  1. Go to Google and search for the name of your game followed by "SWF file".
  2. Look for the game on GitHub and download the SWF file from the code/releases section.
  3. Upload the SWF file below by clicking the Upload button and it will automatically play the game!
  4. Have fun!
